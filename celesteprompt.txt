10. WRITING STYLE RULES — THE MOST IMPORTANT
SECTION
These rules exist specifically to keep AI detection scores low and readability scores high.
Rule 1 — Break sentence rhythm constantly Mix very short sentences (4 to 8 words) with
longer ones (20 to 30 words). Never write three sentences of the same length in a row.
Rule 2 — Never open a paragraph with a topic sentence AI always opens with "The X
segment has seen Y.
" Start instead with a data point, a buyer situation, a direct opinion, or
mid-thought observation.
Rule 3 — State opinions plainly "Buyers who equate luxury with a Gurgaon postcode are
limiting their options unnecessarily.
" That is a consultant's opinion. Write those. They lower
AI scores and build trust simultaneously.
Rule 4 — Address buyer frustrations directly Real buyers are confused by portals,
worried about delays, unsure about developers. Acknowledge this. Write sentences like "A
lot of buyers spend three weekends on portals and end up more confused than when they
started.
"
Rule 5 — No passive constructions Every verb must be active and direct. Not "it has been
noted" or "buyers are advised.
" The consultant is speaking, not reporting.
Rule 6 — No em dashes anywhere Zero em dashes in any content. Use commas, full
stops, or restructure the sentence.
Rule 7 — No AI filler phrases No "it is worth noting,
" "it goes without saying,
" "needless to
say,
" "in conclusion,
" "to summarise,
" "plays a crucial role,
" "serves as a testament.
"
Rule 8 — Data must earn its place Every statistic or data point must be followed by what it
means for the buyer. Never drop a number and move on. E.g.
"Prices rose 98% since 2020.
That happened before the airport opened. The remaining triggers have not priced in yet.
"
Rule 9 — Vary paragraph openings across the entire page Look at the first word of every
paragraph. No two paragraphs in the same section should start the same way.
Rule 10 — Write for the specific market Reference actual sectors, actual developers,
actual expressways, actual RERA context. Generic NCR content is not acceptable. Sector
Alpha, Greater Noida West, Techzone 4, FNG Expressway, Aqua Metro, Jewar Airport,
GNIDA, YEIDA — these are the real signals of market knowledge.
