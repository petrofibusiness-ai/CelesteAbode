### FAQ 4

**Answer**: `Property values in Lucknow have shown consistent appreciation, especially in well-planned sectors and areas with good connectivity. **ID**: `lucknow-possession-delays`

**Answer**: `Possession delays can occur, but RERA has significantly improved project delivery timelines. We help you verify project timelines, track construction progress, and ensure you're protected under RERA regulations. Our team also checks developer track records before recommending projects.`

**Answer**: `Essential documents include: RERA Registration Certificate, Building Plan Approval from Lucknow Development Authority, Title Deed, Encumbrance Certificate, Occupancy Certificate (for ready properties), and NOC from various authorities.
